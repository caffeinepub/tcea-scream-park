# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Add three new attractions to TCEA Scream Park: Bee's Dance Show in Kid Grove, Soak Kingdom water park opening in 2040, and a 1 AM Farewell Procession special event.

**Planned changes:**
- Add Bee's Dance Show as a new attraction in Kid Grove themed land with bees performing dance routines
- Add Soak Kingdom water park scheduled for 2040 with 4 water slides (1 tornado slide + 3 additional slide types)
- Add 1 AM Farewell Procession as a special nightly event occurring after park closes on select nights, featuring characters, floats, and theatrical elements
- Display all three new attractions in appropriate frontend sections (Kid Grove timeline, 2040 timeline, shows/events section)

**User-visible outcome:** Visitors can view details about Bee's Dance Show in the Kid Grove section, see Soak Kingdom water park planned for 2040 with its 4 slides, and learn about the 1 AM Farewell Procession special event in the shows/events section.
