# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Add three new show entries (Waterworks Stunt Show, Dog Show, and Halfway to Horror Nights) to the Shows section of the TCEA Scream Park site, with custom banner images for two of them.

**Planned changes:**
- Add "Waterworks Stunt Show" to the shows content array with a description highlighting jet skis, fire, stunt fighting combat, and a "Coming 2027" year tag (non-haunt-season show)
- Add "Dog Show" to the shows content array with a family-friendly description covering performing dogs and a post-show meet & greet/petting experience, with a "Coming 2029" year tag
- Add "Halfway to Horror Nights" to the shows content array with a description covering the Horror Nights origin story, masks showcase, and annual May timing
- Register generated banner images for Waterworks Stunt Show and Halfway to Horror Nights in the generatedImages map
- Update ShowsSection to display the banner images on Waterworks Stunt Show and Halfway to Horror Nights cards; Dog Show card renders gracefully without a custom image

**User-visible outcome:** Three new show cards appear in the Shows section — Waterworks Stunt Show (with jet ski banner), Dog Show (no custom image), and Halfway to Horror Nights (with atmospheric horror banner).
